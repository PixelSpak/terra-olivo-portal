Laconiko is a prestigious, award-winning Greek family estate producing outstanding extra virgin olive oil in the coastal Trinisa area of Laconia. Focusing exclusively on the robust, high-polyphenol Koroneiki olive, the fourth-generation producers hand-harvest and mill their crop to yield exceptionally low-acidity oils. With hundreds of international awards and global reach, Laconiko is celebrated worldwide for its supreme quality, aromatic profile, and profound health benefits.
