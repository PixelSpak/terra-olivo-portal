AKG Zeytincilik Gıda Tarım is a distinguished Turkish olive oil producer, famously known for its "Granpa" brand. The company operates a sprawling 240-hectare single-estate olive grove in the Muğla province of southwestern Turkey. Driven by an early harvest of exceptional quality that inspired the institutionalization of their brand, AKG Zeytincilik produces world-class, multi-award-winning extra virgin olive oils, notably from the regional Memecik olive variety.
