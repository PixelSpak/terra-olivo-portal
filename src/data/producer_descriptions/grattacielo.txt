GrattaCielo is a multi-award-winning artisanal olive oil producer based in the high-altitude region of Minas Gerais, Brazil. The name "GrattaCielo," meaning "skyscraper" in Italian, reflects the groves' location 1,760 meters above sea level. They craft highly acclaimed Arbosana, Arbequina, and Koroneiki extra virgin olive oils that highlight the growing international prestige of Brazilian olive oil.
