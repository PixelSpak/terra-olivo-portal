F.lli Ruata S.p.A., operating under the renowned brand "Goccia d'Oro," is a historic family-owned Italian company based in Baldissero d'Alba, Piedmont. Founded in 1929, the company blends three generations of artisanal olive selection with highly advanced, certified bottling technology to produce premium extra virgin and D.O.P. olive oils. Their commitment to quality and extensive export capabilities have established them as a reliable, high-standard supplier in the global food processing and culinary markets.
