Florence Olives Ltd represents the niche yet emerging presence of the olive oil market in Kenya, a region where most high-quality olive oil is curated and imported rather than locally cultivated. Operating within a robust edible oils industry, producers and distributors like Florence Olives strive to bring authentic, healthy olive oil experiences to East Africa. While large-scale local cultivation is rare, such enterprises bridge the gap between Mediterranean tradition and Kenyan culinary enthusiasts, emphasizing quality sourcing and distribution.
