Mesogis Zeytin is a boutique Turkish producer based in the Kuyucak district of Aydın, utilizing olives sourced from the ancient lands of the Aegean region. Focusing on traditional, sustainable practices, they produce early-harvest, cold-pressed extra virgin olive oils under the "Olikarnas" brand, primarily using Memecik, Domat, and Eşek varieties. Their premium, high-polyphenol oils have received notable international recognition at competitions like NYIOOC, JOOP, and ATHENA.
