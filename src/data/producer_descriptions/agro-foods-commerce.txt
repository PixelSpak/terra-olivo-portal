Agro Foods & Commerce, SLU, operating under the Priordei brand, is located in the Priorat region of Catalonia, Spain. Led by the Ciuraneta family—whose olive-growing legacy spans over ten generations—the company cultivates Arbequina and Rojal varieties within the pristine Sierra de Montsant Natural Park. Through a strategic alliance with the Dcoop cooperative group, Priordei blends traditional terrace farming methods with a modern vision to elevate their award-winning, distinctive extra virgin olive oils on the global stage.
