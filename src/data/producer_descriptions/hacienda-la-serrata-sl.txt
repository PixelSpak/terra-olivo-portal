Hacienda La Serrata is a prestigious estate in Villena, Alicante, Spain, producing world-class oils under Bodegas Francisco Gómez. The producer crafts organic, vegan, and kosher-certified extra virgin olive oils from early-harvested varieties like Arbequina, Picual, and Cornicabra. Their premium labels frequently win international gold medals for their aromatic complexity, balance, and high polyphenol content.
