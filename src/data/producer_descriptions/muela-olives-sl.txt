Established over 80 years ago, Muela-Olives S.L. (Mueloliva) is a renowned family-owned producer based in Priego de Córdoba, in Spain's Andalusian Subbetica mountains. Combining traditional wisdom with modern cold-pressing technology without heat or water, they craft exceptional extra virgin olive oils like Venta del Barón and Mueloliva Picuda. As a global leader exporting to over 80 countries, their dedication to quality has earned hundreds of international awards.
