Meshek Dagan is a family-owned farm located in Moshav Natur in the southern Golan Heights, Israel, led by third-generation farmer Yuval Dagan. Combining traditional practices with expertise from the University of Jaén, the farm specializes in premium single-varietal extra virgin olive oils like Picual, Coratina, and Barnea. Their cold-pressed oils are treated with the care of fine wine, earning high rankings in international competitions like TerraOlivo.
