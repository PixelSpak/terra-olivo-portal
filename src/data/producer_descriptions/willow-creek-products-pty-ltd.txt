Situated in the scenic Nuy Valley near Worcester, Willow Creek is one of the largest and most celebrated producers of extra virgin olive oil in South Africa. Cultivating a diverse range of olive varieties including Frantoio and Coratina, the estate employs state-of-the-art cold extraction technology to produce vibrant, award-winning oils ranging from delicate to intense. Through sustainable practices and their "Partnering Farms" initiative, Willow Creek consistently delivers premium quality oils while supporting the local agricultural community.
