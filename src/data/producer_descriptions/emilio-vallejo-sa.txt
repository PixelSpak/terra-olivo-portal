Emilio Vallejo, S.A., operating as Aceites Vallejo, is a leading family-owned olive oil producer and bottler established in Jaén, Andalusia—the heart of Spain's olive oil country. Tracing its roots back to 1967, the company blends generations of farming expertise with modern milling and bottling facilities. They export a diverse range of high-quality olive oils to over 40 countries, adhering to strict international certifications to deliver exceptional Spanish flavors globally.
