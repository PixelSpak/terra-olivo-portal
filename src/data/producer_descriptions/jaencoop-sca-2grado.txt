Jaencoop S.C.A. 2º Grado is a powerhouse in the global olive oil industry, functioning as a major cooperative representing thousands of farmers across Jaén, Andalusia, Spain. Focusing primarily on the region's famous Picual olives, the cooperative balances vast production scale with rigorous quality controls. Their premium extra virgin olive oil brands, such as Prólogo, are celebrated globally for their robust flavor profiles and commitment to sustainable agricultural practices.
