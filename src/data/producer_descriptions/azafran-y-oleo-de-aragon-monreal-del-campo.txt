Azafran y Oleo de Aragon, situated in the historic saffron-producing hub of Monreal del Campo, Spain, specializes in a unique gourmet fusion that honors its region's rich heritage. The producer masterfully infuses delicate local saffron threads into smooth Arbequina extra virgin olive oil, yielding an aromatic and vibrantly colored delicacy. This exceptional marriage of Teruel's prized spice and high-quality EVOO creates a deeply flavorful oil that perfectly exemplifies the distinctive gastronomic traditions of Aragon.
