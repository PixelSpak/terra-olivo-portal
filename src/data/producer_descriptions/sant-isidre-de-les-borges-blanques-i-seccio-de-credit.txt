Founded in 1912 in Catalonia, Spain, Cooperativa Sant Isidre de les Borges Blanques produces high-quality extra virgin olive oil under the brand Terrall. Their premium oils are crafted exclusively from the Arbequina olive variety and hold the prestigious Protected Designation of Origin (PDO) Les Garrigues certification. Utilizing traditional cold-pressing methods alongside modern technology, they create award-winning olive oils without additives.
