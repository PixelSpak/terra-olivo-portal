Kochen Azeites Saborizados is an acclaimed Brazilian artisanal producer specializing in exceptionally crafted flavored olive oils from Minas Gerais. Founded by an international olive oil sommelier, the brand uses organic, entirely natural ingredients to infuse oils with vibrant flavors like Pesto, Thyme, and Black Truffle. Celebrated worldwide, Kochen’s innovative gastronomic oils regularly win prestigious international gold medals at competitions like the EVO IOOC.
