Ezio Poggio is primarily renowned as a masterful winemaker in Italy's Colli Tortonesi region of Piedmont, particularly celebrated for revitalizing the ancient Timorasso grape. While the "Poggio" name is widely shared among famous Italian agricultural estates, any olive oil from Ezio Poggio represents an artisanal, boutique extension of his meticulous farming philosophy. His agricultural practices focus on indigenous varieties and terroir-driven quality, reflecting the rich culinary heritage of the Piedmont landscape.
