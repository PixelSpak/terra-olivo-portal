Castillo de Canena is a prestigious family-owned estate in Jaén, Andalusia, with a rich heritage dating back to 1780. Focused on regenerative agriculture and sustainability, they expertly craft cold-pressed extra virgin "olive juice" from their own meticulously tended groves. Specializing in exceptional Picual, Arbequina, and Royal varieties, their globally recognized oils are a true testament to modern gourmet quality and tradition.
