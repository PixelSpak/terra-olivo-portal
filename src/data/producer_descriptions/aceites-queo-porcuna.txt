Located in the historic olive-growing town of Porcuna in Jaén, Spain, Aceites Queo represents the fourth generation of a family dedicated to the olive trade since the 1930s. Transitioning in 2021 to marketing their own premium brand, the three Barrionuevo Torres brothers named their enterprise "Queo"—a local term meaning tranquility. They specialize in early-harvest Picual extra virgin olive oil, combining deep-rooted traditional knowledge with modern, sustainable practices.
