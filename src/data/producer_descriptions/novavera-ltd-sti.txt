Based in the Ayvalık region of Turkey, NovaVera is an acclaimed family-owned producer dedicated to sustainable, organic farming. Utilizing high-precision, cold-pressing techniques below 22°C, they ensure their oils retain maximum phenolic compounds and antioxidants. Renowned for exceptional monovarietal oils like Ayvalık and Memecik, NovaVera consistently achieves top placements in the EVOO World Ranking.
