Ben Shushan is a boutique olive oil producer based in Israel, a country celebrated for its rich agricultural history and exceptional Mediterranean terroir. The family-run estate is dedicated to cultivating high-quality olives, ensuring that every harvest reflects the vibrant, sun-drenched characteristics of the local landscape. By employing careful, small-batch extraction techniques, Ben Shushan produces premium extra virgin olive oil characterized by its balanced flavor profile, vibrant aromas, and deep connection to Israeli farming traditions.
