Founded in 1997 by the Raspagliesi family, Agropecuaria El Mistol S.A. is an acclaimed Argentine producer located at the foot of the Andes Mountains in San Juan province. Named after a century-old Mistol tree on their "Don Mario" farm, the company cultivated 350 hectares of modern olive groves in a terroir uniquely suited for low-acidity extra virgin olive oils. Their premium blends, featuring varieties like Arbequina, Coratina, and Arauco, have garnered numerous prestigious international awards for their exceptional quality and flavor profile.
