Originating from a family business established in 1964, Agrovim S.A. is a prominent Greek exporter based in Kalamata, within the globally renowned Messenia region of the Peloponnese. Famous for their flagship "ILIADA" brand, the company specializes in PDO Kalamata extra virgin olive oils and olives, primarily utilizing the prized Koroneiki variety. By partnering with thousands of local small-scale farmers and utilizing state-of-the-art, internationally certified processing facilities, Agrovim successfully shares Greece's rich olive-growing heritage with over 50 countries worldwide.
