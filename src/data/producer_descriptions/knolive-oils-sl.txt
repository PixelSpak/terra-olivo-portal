Knolive Oils is an internationally celebrated producer situated in the heart of the Subbética Cordobesa Natural Park in Andalusia, Spain. Operating entirely hand-harvested groves on mountainous terrain, they are famed for their "Knolive Epicure," a premium extra virgin blend of Hojiblanca and Picuda. Characterized by fresh notes of green apple and almond, their oils consistently top global rankings and symbolize the ultimate Mediterranean lifestyle.
