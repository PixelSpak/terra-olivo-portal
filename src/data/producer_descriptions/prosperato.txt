Prosperato is a pioneering, family-owned Brazilian extra virgin olive oil producer operating out of Rio Grande do Sul since 2013. Employing an artisanal, single-estate approach from seedling to bottling, they craft both premium blends and exquisite monovarietal oils using state-of-the-art Italian machinery. Recognized as one of Brazil's most awarded brands, their remarkably fresh oils have garnered prestigious international accolades, including top medals at the NYIOOC.
