The Stathorakis Olive Oil Mill, located in the Moires region of Heraklion, Crete, is a third-generation family business renowned for its "Agroulida" brand. Dedicated exclusively to the native Koroneiki olive, the mill utilizes cold extraction and modern standards to create an oil that is deeply rooted in natural and sustainable cultivation. The resulting extra virgin olive oil, recognized at major international competitions, beautifully reflects the pure, unadulterated character of the Cretan landscape.
