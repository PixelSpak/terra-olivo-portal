OPG Nives Morović is a highly acclaimed Croatian producer based in Žman on the island of Dugi Otok, famed for their centuries-old olive cultivation dating back to 1607. Their ecological, award-winning brand 'Nai 3.3' produces premium extra virgin olive oils, notably their globally recognized Coratina variety. Processed in their own high-tech mill on the same day as harvest, their oils are celebrated for exceptional quality and high polyphenol content.
