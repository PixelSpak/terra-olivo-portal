Situated in Albalate del Arzobispo within Spain's historic Bajo Aragón region, Almazara Artal is a celebrated family-run olive mill managed by Arantxa Garín. The mill is dedicated to crafting exceptional extra virgin olive oils, such as their award-winning "El Aceite de Marta" and "Safeito," primarily using the region's signature Empeltre and Arbequina varieties. Operating under the rigorous Aceite del Bajo Aragón PDO, they employ meticulously controlled mechanical processes to ensure the highest quality while promoting local oleotourism.
