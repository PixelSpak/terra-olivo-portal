Kavalaria Estate is an award-winning, family-run producer situated in the eastern foothills of Mount Psiloritis in Crete, Greece. Owned by Georgios Saridakis, the estate is famous for its "Pathos" extra virgin olive oil, an organic, early-harvest product made exclusively from 100% Koroneiki olives. Cold-pressed within hours of hand-harvesting, this high-polyphenol oil is celebrated internationally for its incredibly low acidity and complex, grassy flavor profile.
