P.M. Matsoukas Ltd is an award-winning producer of organic extra virgin olive oil located in the village of Athienou in Cyprus's Mesaoria region. Established in 2011 to honor a long-standing family tradition, they focus on cultivating the Koroneiki olive variety using strictly organic, pesticide-free practices. Their meticulously cold-pressed oils are renowned for their low acidity and exceptional quality, earning recognition at international competitions.
