Azeite Batalha is a prominent, award-winning olive oil producer situated in Pinheiro Machado within the Pampa region of Rio Grande do Sul, Brazil. Located on the 31st parallel—an ideal latitude for olive cultivation—the estate employs sustainable farming practices to cultivate renowned varieties like Koroneiki, Arbequina, and Picual. With a steadfast focus on creating world-class products, Batalha crafts intensely flavorful and complex extra virgin olive oils that stand as a proud testament to the emerging excellence of the Brazilian olive oil industry.
