Otto Olivas de Gramado Ltda is a prominent olive oil producer and agricultural tourist destination nestled in the Serra Gaúcha region of Brazil. Managing over 12,000 olive trees across 29 hectares, they cultivate varieties like Arbequina and Koroneiki to create their globally recognized, award-winning 'Terroir Serrano' extra virgin olive oil. Their estate blends high-quality olive production with a rich cultural and gastronomic visitor experience.
