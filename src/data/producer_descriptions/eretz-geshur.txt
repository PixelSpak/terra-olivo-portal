Eretz Geshur, located on Kibbutz Geshur in the southern Golan Heights of Israel, is a premier producer of high-quality extra virgin olive oil. Cultivating a diverse range of local and international varietals like Barnea and Koroneiki, they meticulously control their mechanical harvesting and cold-pressing using an on-site modern mill. Renowned for freshness and environmental sustainability, their award-winning oils reflect the unique terroir of the Golan Heights and are celebrated in both domestic and international competitions.
