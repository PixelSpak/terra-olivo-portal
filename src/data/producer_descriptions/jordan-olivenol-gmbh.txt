Jordan Olivenöl GmbH is a unique family enterprise headquartered in Germany that cultivates and produces outstanding extra virgin olive oil on the Greek island of Lesbos. Focusing on traditional, sustainable, and no-irrigation farming of Kolovi and Adramitiana olives, they process their harvest using an unfiltered system to retain a naturally mild and fruity profile. Their multi-award-winning oil is highly prized by professional chefs and gourmets across Europe.
