Ptora Family Boutique is a prestigious, family-owned Israeli olive farm founded in 1956 in the Lachish region, resting above an ancient Byzantine city. Guided by internationally certified olive oil judge Ido Tamir, they produce an exceptional array of single-varietal and blended extra virgin olive oils from both local and international olive varieties. Their dedication to heritage and craftsmanship has established them as a premier Israeli brand, consistently earning top international awards.
