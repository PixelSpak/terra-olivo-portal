Il Fontanaro is a traditional organic family estate in Paciano, Umbria, Italy, renowned for its signature "Olio della Pace" (Source of Peace). Hand-picking a blend of Leccino, Frantoio, Moraiolo, and other local varieties, the estate mills its olives within hours to produce incredibly fresh, award-winning oil. With its golden-green hue and complex spicy-bitter notes, their extra virgin olive oil frequently secures top spots in international guides like Flos Olei.
