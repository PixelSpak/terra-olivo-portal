Olioinversiones Glam S.A. operates Finca Rumaroli, a premier olive estate in Coronel Dorrego, Buenos Aires, Argentina. Cultivating diverse varieties like Arbequina, Coratina, and Frantoio across multiple terroirs, the company produces acclaimed monovarietal and blended extra virgin olive oils. Their prestigious brands, Garbo and Rumaroli, have earned significant recognition at global competitions, including the EVOO World Ranking and London Olive Oil Awards.
