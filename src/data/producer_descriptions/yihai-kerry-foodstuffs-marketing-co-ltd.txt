While Yihai Kerry Foodstuffs is a major Chinese food processing conglomerate, it plays a vital commercial role in the Spanish olive oil industry as a leading importer and distributor. Through its widely recognized "Olivoilà" brand, the company brings high-quality Spanish extra virgin olive oil to the Chinese market. Emphasizing Mediterranean origins and health benefits, Yihai Kerry serves as a crucial bridge connecting Spanish olive oil production with an expansive international consumer base.
