Yağmur Organik Ürünler is a prominent Turkish producer best known for its premium "Ninurta" olive oil brand. Sourcing exceptional olive varieties such as Edremit and Trilye, the company crafts high-quality monovarietal and blended extra virgin olive oils. Recognized consistently at prestigious international competitions like OLIVE JAPAN and the London Olive Oil Awards, Yağmur Organik is celebrated for its dedication to organic practices and exceptional quality.
