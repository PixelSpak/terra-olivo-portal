Kibbutz Shomria, also known as Meshek Shomriya, is a respected Israeli agricultural producer cultivating olive groves in the country's south. They offer a diverse, high-quality line of extra virgin olive oils, including popular varietals like Koroneiki, Arbequina, and the intensely spicy Coratina. Praised for their distinct and versatile flavor profiles, their oils are frequently recognized in major industry competitions and rankings.
