El Aceite de Marta is a premium extra virgin olive oil crafted by the family-owned Almazara Artal in the Bajo Aragón region of Spain. Utilizing traditional varieties like Empeltre and Arbequina, the producer controls every step from harvest to bottling using strictly mechanical procedures. Their commitment to avoiding ground contact and ensuring freshness has earned them numerous international accolades, including Grand Prestige Gold at the TerraOlivo IOOC.
