Olivapalacios, S.L. is a family-owned producer situated in the nutrient-rich, volcanic soils of Almagro, Ciudad Real, Spain. Managing nearly 300 hectares of groves and an on-site mill, they produce the internationally celebrated "Palacio de los Olivos" brand. Emphasizing sustainability and early harvesting, their Picual, Arbequina, and Arbosana extra virgin olive oils consistently rank among the best in the world.
