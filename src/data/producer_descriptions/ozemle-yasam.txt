Özem'le Yaşam is a premium Turkish olive oil brand based in the Ayvalık/Gömeç region of Balıkesir, dedicated to sustainable agriculture and clean eating. They specialize in high-quality, cold-pressed extra virgin olive oils, particularly early harvest varieties prized for their intense aroma and high polyphenol content. Led by an olive oil tasting expert, the brand also promotes agro-tourism, offering authentic Aegean culinary and educational experiences.
