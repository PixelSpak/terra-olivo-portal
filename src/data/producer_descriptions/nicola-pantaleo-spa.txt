Nicola Pantaleo S.p.A. is a prominent family-run Italian producer with roots dating back to 1890, located in Fasano di Puglia, Brindisi. Producing around 12,000 tons of oil annually, they combine traditional Pugliese methods with modern packaging standards. Their diverse portfolio includes high-quality extra virgin, organic, and flavored olive oils, establishing a strong international presence in markets worldwide.
