Balija is a highly acclaimed, award-winning producer of extra virgin olive oil nestled in the idyllic coastal town of Fažana, Istria, Croatia. Established by the Balija family in 1989, the estate crafts harmonious signature blends from cherished local olive varieties such as Buža, Istarska Bjelica, and Karbonaca. Consistently recognized by prestigious global guides like Flos Olei, Balija’s uncompromising approach to quality yields exceptional, limited-production oils that beautifully embody the vibrant, natural Mediterranean ecosystem of the Istrian peninsula.
