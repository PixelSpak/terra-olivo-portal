Eleourgiki S.A. M. G. Gkoutzoumits A.B.E.E is a dedicated Greek olive oil producer, reflecting the rich agricultural heritage of Greece's local mills. Drawing on the long-standing "Eleourgiki" cooperative tradition of quality olive working, the producer focuses on regional cultivation and authentic extraction methods. Their operations underscore the importance of local Greek varietals to deliver robust, traditional extra virgin olive oils to the market.
