Via Viv is a distinguished olive oil brand founded by Vivian van Wordragen, a Dutch olive oil expert and certified professional taster. Sourcing premium extra virgin olive oils from renowned regions such as Italy, the brand is dedicated to offering exceptionally high-quality and award-winning robust oils. Beyond curating exquisite olive oils, Via Viv emphasizes education through masterclasses and tastings, elevating the appreciation of fine olive oils for both chefs and discerning consumers.
