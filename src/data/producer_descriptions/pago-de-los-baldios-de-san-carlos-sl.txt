Pago de los Baldíos de San Carlos S.L. is a distinguished Spanish olive oil estate situated in the Tiétar Valley of Extremadura. Benefiting from a unique microclimate that allows for an exceptionally early harvest, they produce award-winning, minimally processed extra virgin olive oils primarily from the Arbequina variety. Their premium products, characterized by remarkably low acidity and vibrant fruity aromas, are favored by Michelin-starred chefs and gourmet culinary establishments worldwide.
