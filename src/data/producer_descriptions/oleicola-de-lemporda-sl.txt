Located in Rabós d'Empordà, Catalonia, Oleícola de l'Empordà S.L. operates within the "Oli de l'Empordà" Protected Designation of Origin. Their flagship Clos de la Torre extra virgin olive oil is crafted exclusively from estate-grown native varieties like Argudell and Arbequina. Marrying traditional manual methods with modern technology, their premium oils have garnered numerous awards at international competitions such as TerraOlivo.
