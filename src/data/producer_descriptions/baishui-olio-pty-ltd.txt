Baishui Olio Pty Ltd is a dedicated olive oil producer located in Australia, an area renowned for its innovative agricultural practices and strong commitment to quality. The company focuses on cultivating premium olive varieties to craft high-quality extra virgin olive oils that highlight the unique Australian terroir. Through a careful balance of sustainable farming and modern extraction methods, Baishui Olio produces beautifully balanced, flavorful oils suited for both gourmet applications and everyday culinary uses.
