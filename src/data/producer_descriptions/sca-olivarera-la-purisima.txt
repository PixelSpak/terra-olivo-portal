S.C.A. Olivarera La Purísima refers to notable Andalusian olive oil cooperatives, with the most famous situated in Priego de Córdoba, operating since 1945. Under the strict D.O.P. Priego de Córdoba certification, they produce the highly acclaimed, multi-award-winning extra virgin olive oil brand "El Empiedro." These cooperatives reflect the deep-rooted tradition and exceptional quality of Spanish olive oil production.
