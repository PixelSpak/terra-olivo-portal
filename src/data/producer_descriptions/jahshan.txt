Jahshan is a historic, family-operated Israeli producer with olive oil-making roots in the Galilee region dating back to 1863. Operating an advanced mill and visitor center at Moshav Kalanit, the seventh-generation business produces widely respected extra virgin olive oils known for low acidity and traditional cold-pressing. Their high-quality oils, alongside a variety of authentic Galilean culinary products, proudly bear the official Israeli olive oil quality mark.
