Situated in Neot Golan in the Golan Heights, Meshek Inbal is a boutique producer of premium extra virgin olive oil benefiting from the region's rich basalt soil. The farm emphasizes modern agricultural practices, including precision pruning and mechanized harvesting, to maximize oil quality and aroma. Their exceptional craftsmanship is highlighted by their Koroneiki variety, which was awarded "Best Koroneiki" at the TerraOlivo international competition.
