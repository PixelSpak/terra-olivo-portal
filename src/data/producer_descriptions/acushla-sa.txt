Established in 2006 by fashion entrepreneur Joaquim Moreira, Acushla is situated on the 300-hectare Quinta do Prado estate in the Trás-os-Montes e Alto Douro region of northeastern Portugal. The brand, whose Celtic name translates to "beat of my heart," focuses on high-quality, organic extra virgin olive oil from native varieties like Cobrançosa and Madural. Acushla distinguishes itself through an uncompromising commitment to sustainability, utilizing organic farming, solar power, and operating an advanced mill with top-tier food safety certifications.
