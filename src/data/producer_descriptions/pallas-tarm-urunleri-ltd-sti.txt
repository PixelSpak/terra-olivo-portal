Pallas Tarım Ürünleri LTD ŞTİ is an esteemed Turkish olive oil producer based in the major cultivation hub of Manisa. Established in 2017, the company is dedicated to creating elite, early-harvest extra virgin olive oils primarily using the premium Arbequina olive variety. Certified organic and renowned for extremely low acidity and high polyphenol content, their meticulously crafted oils consistently win top honors at prestigious international competitions like the NYIOOC.
