Hailing from the fertile Northern region of Israel, Adama Boutique is a renowned producer of premium extra virgin olive oil, celebrated for its artisanal and sustainable farming methods. Unrelated to the large agrochemical company of the same name, this boutique producer frequently garners acclaim in international competitions, including the EVOO World Ranking. They are especially recognized for crafting distinctive varietals such as Pichuline, emphasizing quality and tradition in every harvest.
