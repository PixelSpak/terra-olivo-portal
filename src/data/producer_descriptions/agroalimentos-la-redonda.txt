Based in the Jalón Valley of Aragón, Spain, Agroalimentos La Redonda is a family-owned enterprise with over 35 years of agricultural expertise. Operating from their 200-hectare estate in La Almunia de Doña Godina, the company inaugurated its own state-of-the-art mill in 2004 to ensure complete traceability and superior quality. Specializing in the Arbequina variety, they meticulously control the entire production process from harvest to bottling, crafting award-winning extra virgin olive oils celebrated for their innovation and regional character.
