Monva S.L. is a family-owned producer founded in 1972, located in the Sierra Mágina Natural Park in Jaén, Spain. They produce estate-grown extra virgin olive oils, carefully controlling the process from cultivation on their 650-hectare Cortijo Virgen de los Milagros estate to their on-site mill. Specializing in the Picual variety, their renowned brands like Dominus and Montabes carry the Sierra Mágina Protected Designation of Origin and international quality certifications.
