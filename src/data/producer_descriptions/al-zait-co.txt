Al-Zait & Co. is a premium producer of Brazilian extra virgin olive oil, operating out of the Estância Santa Leonida in Rosário do Sul, Rio Grande do Sul. This region is considered the heartland of Brazil's burgeoning olive oil industry due to its favorable winter climate. Emphasizing sustainable agriculture and rapid cold-pressing techniques, Al-Zait has gained significant international prestige—frequently medaling in global competitions for its outstanding monocultivar oils like Picual and Koroneiki.
