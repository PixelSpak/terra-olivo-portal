Finca Las Manillas is a historic family-owned olive farm and mill located in Arquillos, within Spain's renowned Jaén province. Managed by the fourth generation of the family, the estate focuses on sustainable and integrated agriculture to produce exceptional, early-harvest Extra Virgin Olive Oils from Picual and Arbequina varieties. Sold under brands like "Noviembre," their oils emphasize terroir, quality, and community, highlighted by their active oleotourism center that welcomes visitors to experience their traditional yet innovative craft.
