Vassilakis Estate is a historic, family-run olive oil producer situated in the picturesque Mirambello Valley of Crete, Greece, with a legacy spanning back to 1865. Focusing primarily on the indigenous Koroneiki olive, the estate utilizes careful cold-extraction methods to craft premium and organic extra virgin olive oils known for their high antioxidant content and peppery finish. The acclaimed estate offers immersive visitor experiences and exports its award-winning "Charisma" and estate lines worldwide.
